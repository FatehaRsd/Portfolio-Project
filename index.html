About

A Return to Code: Combining Experience with Renewed Zeal

My love for learning sparked my coding journey during my undergraduate years. I spent four years learning Java and building a strong foundation before entering the workforce. My internship was a launchpad for my coding journey, with PHP as my first fuel. I kept exploring and learning, and even landed a software tester role before graduation! It was a great way to showcase my skills before hitting the ground running. After graduation, I transitioned to mobile networking and construction of telecommunication structure. While I learned valuable project management and problem-solving skills in this complex field, the pull of code never faded. I craved the creative freedom and seeing my code make a real difference in the world.

Through introspection, I realized my diverse skills and desire to contribute to society could make a bigger difference. This is not just a return to coding; it's a rekindled passion, a reunion of skills and purpose. I'm eager to find a path where my technical expertise can fuel meaningful projects, drive positive change, and make a real impact. A passionate learner with diverse technical expertise and a relentless drive to grow, I'm ready for a vibrant journey fueled by code and purpose. I invite you to explore my potential and discover how my skills can contribute to your vision.
